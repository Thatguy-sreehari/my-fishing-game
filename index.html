How to Play

🎣 Basic Controls:

  • Cast Line: Click the "CAST LINE" button to try fishing
  • Advance Time: Click "ADVANCE TIME" to move through the day (affects fish availability)
  • Choose Zone: Select Freshwater or Saltwater fishing zones

🌙 Night Fishing (3 AM - 6 AM):

  • Special fish only appear at night!
  • These include mystic creatures like Ghost Fish, Anglerfish, and the legendary Void Fish
  • Night catches typically have higher point values

📊 Fish Statistics:

  • Size: Tiny, Small, Medium, Large (affects rarity)
  • Rarity: Common, Uncommon, Rare, Very Rare (affects points)
  • Points: Each fish has different point values (10-100 points)

🏆 Goal:

  • Catch all 102 unique fish species!
  • Collect thousands of points
  • Discover special night-only creatures
