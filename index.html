HOW TO PLAY

Welcome to Cat Goes Deep

A game combining Cat Goes Fishing charm with Scale the Depths strategy. Catch all 102 fish species in the world!

Basic Controls

  • Cast Line: Try to catch a fish at your current location
  • Advance Time: Move through the day cycle (affects fish availability)
  • View Inventory: Check your caught fish and manage collection

Fishing Mechanics

  • Different locations have different fish species
  • Time of day affects which fish appear (day vs night fishing)
  • Water type determines available species
  • Rarer fish are harder to catch but worth more points

Depth System (Scale the Depths)

  • Each catch increases your depth level
  • Deeper levels unlock rarer fish
  • Higher depth = better rewards

Fish Rarity Tiers

Common - +10-30 points
Uncommon - +20-50 points
Rare - +50-80 points
Legendary - +85-100 points

Night Fishing

Between 3 AM - 6 AM, special mystical fish appear. These rare creatures have unique appearances and high point values!

Tips for Success

  • Visit different locations to expand your collection
  • Fish at night for rare species
  • Check your statistics to see what species you're missing
  • Build your complete collection to achieve 100%
